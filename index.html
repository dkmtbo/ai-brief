EU agrees a "Digital Omnibus" — high-risk AI Act duties slip to December 2027

Council, Parliament and Commission negotiators reached a provisional deal — the first amendments to the AI Act since 2024. High-risk (Annex III) obligations move from August 2026 to December 2027, easing the near-term compliance crunch for firms deploying AI in regulated client work. Transparency rules for AI-generated content, though, tighten to a three-month grace period.
